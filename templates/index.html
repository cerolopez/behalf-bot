What is Behalf Bot? 🤖

Behalf Bot is a GPT-4 chatbot instructed to answer career questions on behalf of its creator, Cecilia Lopez. Its responses are curated specifically for recruiters and hiring managers. Behalf Bot can do a lot, but it's best at making inferences and summarizing information.

Here are some examples of questions you can ask:

  • Can you tell me about Cecilia's experience with prompt engineering?
  • Does Cecilia have experience leading projects?
  • What are some of Cecilia's biggest strengths?
  • Would Cecilia's combination of experiences make her an effective UX engineer?

If you catch any bugs, feel free to send a screenshot to Cecilia at the email in the footer 😊
