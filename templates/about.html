Behalf Bot

About

Inspired by her own conversations with ChatGPT, Cecilia Lopez built Behalf Bot to help recruiters and hiring managers parse through her uniquely diverse background. She used information publicly available on her LinkedIn and website and curated data to inform Behalf Bot's responses and instructed the model how to respond for various use cases, including:

  • Users asking about experience that Cecilia does not have
  • Users asking about experience that Cecilia has different but related experience in
  • Users asking about a quality or skill that can't directly be inferred from Cecilia's LinkedIn referrals
  • Users asking questions a recruiter or hiring manager wouldn't ask
